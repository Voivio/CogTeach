Project description

We are a group of researchers at UC San Diego, focusing on future learning technologies. Current online learning platforms such as video conferencing software (e.g., Zoom) and MOOC platforms are widely used for synchronous and asynchronous online instruction, especially during the global pandemic. In this project, we are trying to develop a next-generation online study platform for better communication between the students and the instructor. Course description

The full curriculum comprises 4 talks plus 1 introductory session covering basic yet essential topics related to artificial intelligence, including the basics of AI, how AI models see and talk, and their influences on our society. The contents covered will support your future exploration in the AI world.
