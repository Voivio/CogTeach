Important Experiment Instructions

Experiment procedure

Before you start: consent forms

Our study offers free AI online courses. You could also receive compensation (maximum: $90) after this study. Note that we only recruit students who do not have or have rare previous experience in AI. If you have rich experience in AI-related techniques like machine learning, please do not join in our study. The study procedure is depicted below.

Before study, first sign the consent form in Consent forms block by clicking eSign button. After you have signed it in DocuSign, check the box stating that By checking this box, I confirm that I have signed the consent form in the study. and click Submit button.

Note: you MUST use your UCSD email to sign the consent form on DocuSign and participate in our study. If you do not sign the consent form or use non-UCSD emails in DocuSign and study, your study results will be removed automatically.

Lectures to complete

The following list is the lectures to be completed. The formal study is composed of Lecture 1 to 4 and two short questionnaires.

  1. Introduction to the system. (~6 mins)
  2. Lecture 1. (~30 mins)
  3. The first questionnaire. (~1 mins)
  4. Lecture 2. (~30 mins)
  5. Lecture 3. (~30 mins)
  6. Lecture 4. (~30 mins)
  7. The second questionnaire. (~1 mins)

Each lecture includes one 30-min course video and two tests of 10 short questions, where one before the lecture and another one after the lecture. You need to finish both tests. Otherwise, the lecture is not viewed as finished and you could not receive compensation for this lecture.

After you finish lecture 1/lecture 4, you need to finish the corresponding questionnaire in How is your experience block. Otherwise, you could not receive compensation for lecture 1/lecture 4.

We suggest you have at least a 10-min rest between lectures.

Warning before study

If you have joined our previous online learning study using this system before Nov 23, you could not participate in this study again. We will check the participant email account in our previous study. Even if you participate in our study again, you will not receive any compensation.

Do not forget these!

  • Chrome, PC, Volume. Do not use any other browsers. Use your computer (not phones, not tablets). Do not forget to open your sound/volume to hear the instructions.
  • Stable Internet. The lecture could not pause and resume. Therefore, make sure your Internet connection is stable and do not refresh your webpage during the lecture. Otherwise, you may have to start from the beginning of the lecture.
  • No Interruption. Make sure you have enough time (30-35 min) without being interrupted before you start each lecture because the lecture could not pause. Otherwise you have to repeat this lecture.
  • Camera, Face. You need to enable your camera on Chrome and show your face without a mask during the courses. If your face could not be detected all the time, you will lose the access to the courses. As depicted in the consent form, your face and eye gaze will only be used for research purposes and will not be released to the public.
  • Video Gets Stuck. If your lecture gets stuck, it may be due to your unstable Internet connection. Please wait for a while and it may resume later. If it could not resume after 10 minutes, please send an email containing the screenshot of your current problem to us and we'll fix it soon.
  • Be Honest. Please do not cheat in the tests since joining in a research study is a serious thing. Our system could detect your cheating. If we find that you cheat during the tests or just make quick and random selections, you will be removed from our courses and receive zero compensation. If you submit your answers multiple times in the tests, we will only use your first submitted answers. If your response time in the tests is abnormal (too fast or too slow), your results will be suspected as cheating as well.
  • Completion. Be sure to remember the 4-digit code that is assigned to you after you finish the pre-test/post-test and submit the code in the corresponding location. If you fail to finish this last step, your current lecture will not be viewed as completion and you have to repeat it if you want to proceed to the next lecture. If you forget the 4-digit code and repeat your pre-test/post-test, please be sure to notify us that you have repeated it via email.

Compensation Policy

  • Finish lecture 1 + the first 1-min questionnaire → $5
  • Finish lecture 1 + lecture 2 → $10
  • Finish lecture 1 + lecture 2 + lecture 3 → $15
  • Finish lecture 1 + lecture 2 + lecture 3 + lecture 4 + the second questionnaire → $20

If you have finished four lectures, you could have the chance to compare your performance in the average accuracy and response time of post-lecture tests of all lectures with other participants. Note that your pre-lecture tests score will not affect your rewards. We will give additional rewards for top N participants in post-test scores, out of the total 40 participants:

Top 1 2 - 3 4 - 8 9 - 15 16 - 25 26 - 40
+$70 +$50 +$25 +$10 +$5 0

So the final compensation for all 40 participants who have finished four lectures is:

Top 1 2 - 3 4 - 8 9 - 15 16 - 25 26 - 40
$90 $70 $45 $30 $25 $20

We will first compare your average accuracy. For participants with the same accuracy, we compare your response time, i.e. how much time you have spent on the tests.

The system will be closed after 40 participants have completed all four lectures so that no other participants could join. For other participants who do not finish all lectures before we close our system, we will provide you with compensation according to your progress, i.e. $5 for each lecture.

Note that you will not receive any compensation if you do not follow study instructions, including:

  • Do not show your full face
  • Cheating
  • The same person to join in the study with different UCSD emails
  • Do not sign the consent form before using our system to learn courses
  • Do not use UCSD emails

Your compensation will be released to your UCSD email in the form of an Amazon e-Gift Card. So make sure you input your UCSD email correctly. After you have completed your study, email soxu@ucsd.edu to confirm your completion and request your compensation.

If you meet technique issues in our system

If your video gets stuck, or the webpage crashes, be sure to capture a screenshot and send it to our email (cogteach.info@gmail.com). If your email does not contain any evidence of your problem such as the screenshot, you will not receive any response.
